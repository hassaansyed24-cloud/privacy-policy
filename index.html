Privacy Policy

Ayah for the Heart
Last Updated: February 27, 2026
Our Commitment: Ayah for the Heart is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We collect minimal data, store everything locally on your device, and never sell or share your information.

1. Information We Collect

We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any data that leaves your device

We DO Store Locally:

  • Saved ayahs: Verses you bookmark are stored only on your device
  • App preferences: Your settings and choices remain private on your device
  • Quiz responses: Stored temporarily during the quiz session only

2. In-App Purchases

When you make a purchase through the app:

  • Apple handles all transactions through the App Store
  • We receive only confirmation that you made a purchase
  • Your payment information is never visible to us
  • Purchases are managed by Apple's privacy policy and terms

3. Data Storage

Everything stays on your device:

  • All data is stored locally using iOS secure storage
  • Nothing is uploaded to external servers
  • No cloud sync or backup (your saved ayahs stay only on your device)
  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data

4. Third-Party Services

We use only Apple's services:

  • StoreKit for in-app purchases (managed by Apple)
  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services
  • No social media integrations
  • No third-party tracking

5. Children's Privacy

Ayah for the Heart is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to anyone. Period.

The only exception is when you explicitly use the "Share" feature to share an ayah with someone — this happens through iOS's native sharing and we don't see or store who you share with.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete data: Unsave any ayah or delete the app entirely
  • No account required: No login, no profile, no tracking
  • Export impossible: Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to export
  • Opt-out unnecessary: There's nothing to opt out of

8. Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS built-in security and encryption
  • Local-only storage (never transmitted over the internet)
  • No servers to be hacked
  • No databases containing your information

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Post the new policy at this same URL
  • Notify you through an app update if the changes are significant

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

Email: hassaansyed24@icloud.com

We typically respond within 48 hours.

Summary

In Plain English:

Ayah for the Heart is a simple, private app. We don't collect your data, we don't track you, we don't show ads, and we don't share anything about you with anyone. Everything you do in the app stays on your phone. That's it. 🕌
